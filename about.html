About Us

ONEWORLD INITIATIVE FOR SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT (OISD) Is a non-governmental, non- partisan, non- profit-making organization conceptualized to galvanize Stakeholders effort towards promoting the culture of peace through dynamic conflict transformation strategies for poverty reduction and sustainable development in Nigeria and Africa in general. Established over a decade ago and duly registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC), OISD has consistently remained at the forefront of advocacy, policy reforms, direct interventions, empowerment, and development initiatives. The organization is dedicated to restoring human dignity and promoting inclusive growth, recognizing that without peace, justice, and equity, society would descend into chaos. Over the years, OISD has built a strong reputation as a trusted partner in peacebuilding and social transformation, working collaboratively with governmental institutions, civil society organizations, and international development partners. Its interventions are designed to empower communities, foster dialogue, and build sustainable systems that ensure stability, social justice, and human progress.

Our Vission

To heal the society of all forms of conflict inimical to our collective prosperity and Development to make the world a better place for us and our children's children.

Our Mission

Develop concept that reframes the way peace building initiatives are discussed and pursued by reviewing the social structure and dynamics behind conflicts, geared towards transforming relationships, interests, discourses and the very negative value in society that supports the continuation of violence.

Intervention Philosophy

OISD believes in the establishment of social protection systems that are proactive, humane, inclusive, and sustainable. These systems are designed to improve the general welfare of people and address both immediate needs and long-term structural challenges.

Advocacy Approach

The organization works to create conditions for dialogue among civilizations, cultures, and peoples based on mutual respect for shared values. Through sustained advocacy, OISD provides platforms where diverse voices can be heard and constructive solutions to conflicts can emerge.

Creed

We envision and respond to the movement and flow of social conflict as life-giving opportunities to create constructive change. Our creed is rooted in reducing violence, increasing restorative justice, and fostering solutions to real-life problems in human relationships through direct interaction and inclusive dialogue.

Our Core Strategies

To deliver on its mandate of promoting peace, reducing poverty, and driving sustainable development, the Oneworld Initiative for Sustainable Development (OISD) employs a set of well-defined strategies. These strategies guide program design, community engagement, and stakeholder collaboration, ensuring that interventions are effective, inclusive, and sustainable.

Research and Needs Assessment

Every intervention begins with evidence. OISD conducts research, baseline studies, and community need assessments to understand the underlying causes of conflict, poverty, and social inequality. By generating reliable data and insights, the organization is able to design programs that respond to the real challenges faced by communities while aligning with national and global development goals.

Capacity Building and Skill Acquisition

Recognizing that lasting change must come from empowered individuals and communities, OISD invests heavily in capacity building. This includes training workshops, skill acquisition programs, mentorship, and technical support for youth, women, and other vulnerable groups. By equipping people with the knowledge and skills needed to thrive, OISD reduces dependency, creates employment opportunities, and builds resilience.

Advocacy and Public Enlighten
ment

Advocacy is central to OISD’s approach. The organization raises awareness on key issues such as peace, human rights, governance, health, and education. Through campaigns, media partnerships, and community dialogues, OISD amplifies the voices of marginalized groups, challenges harmful norms, and promotes policies that foster justice, equality, and sustainable development.

Direct Interven
tion

Beyond advocacy and policy engagement, OISD implements practical interventions that address urgent needs and long-term challenges. These include peacebuilding initiatives, legal aid services, empowerment programs, education and health projects, and humanitarian assistance. By combining immediate support with long-term development strategies, OISD ensures that interventions are both responsive and sustainable.
