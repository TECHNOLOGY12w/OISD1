Programs

International Children’s Day Celebration

We organized the International Children’s Day Celebration in collaboration with government partners, reaching children and families with fun, educational and supportive programming. The 2024 celebration (May 27, 2024) was held in collaboration with the Federal Ministry of Women Affairs and other stakeholders in Abuja.

Asofest (Cultural Festival)

Asofest 2023 was celebrated in collaboration with the FCT Department of Art & Culture (SDS) in Abuja. The program highlighted cultural performances, exhibitions and community engagement activities.

Community Arts & Education

Ongoing arts, mentorship and community outreach programs designed to support children's development, cultural knowledge sharing and stakeholder collaboration across the Federal Capital Territory.
