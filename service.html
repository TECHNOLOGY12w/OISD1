What we do

Oneworld Initiative for Sustainable Development (OISD) is a non-governmental, non-partisan, and non-profit-making organization dedicated to galvanizing stakeholders’ efforts toward promoting a culture of peace and fostering dynamic conflict transformation strategies. Its goal is to promote peace, reduce poverty and drive sustainable development in Nigeria and Africa. To achieve this, OISD works across carefully defined thematic areas that reflect the organization’s mission and vision while addressing the pressing needs of society.

Peace and conflict Resolution/ Transformation...

As conflict remains one of the greatest impediments to sustainable development in Africa, OISD focuses on designing and implementing practical peacebuilding and conflict transformation programs. The organization provides mediation platforms, dialogue forums, and community-based interventions that address the root causes of violence, rebuild broken relationships, and promote coexistence. By reframing conflicts as opportunities for constructive change, OISD contributes to a more peaceful and just society.

Human Right/ Legal Aids...

In recognition of the central role that justice plays in sustainable development, OISD advocates for the protection and promotion of human rights. Through partnerships with legal institutions and civil society organizations, the organization provides legal aid services, supports victims of abuse and discrimination, and ensures that marginalized groups have access to justice. This commitment underscores OISD’s role in upholding human dignity and fostering equality before the law.

Women, Girl Child and Child's law...

The empowerment of women and the protection of children are at the heart of OISD’s development agenda. The organization advocates for gender equality, champions the rights of women and girls, and works to eliminate harmful practices that perpetuate discrimination. Through education, legal advocacy, and empowerment programs, OISD promotes the welfare of women, girls, and children, ensuring that they are protected under the law and equipped to thrive.

Security...

Without security, peace and development cannot be sustained. OISD partners with security agencies, community leaders, and grassroots stakeholders to strengthen community safety and security systems. By promoting non-violent conflict resolution, civic awareness, and joint security initiatives, the organization contributes to reducing insecurity, addressing violence, and fostering safer communities where sustainable development can take root.

Youth Development...

OISD recognizes young people as critical stakeholders in building a peaceful and prosperous society. Its youth development initiatives focus on leadership training, skill acquisition, mentorship, and entrepreneurship programs. By empowering young people with the tools to become productive citizens and peace ambassadors, OISD contributes to reducing unemployment, curbing restiveness, and creating opportunities for inclusive growth.

Culture and Tourism...

OISD leverages culture and tourism as powerful tools for peacebuilding and development. Cultural exchanges and tourism programs are used to promote mutual understanding, celebrate diversity, and foster social cohesion. By integrating culture into its peace strategies, the organization provides innovative approaches to conflict prevention, while tourism initiatives generate livelihood opportunities that reduce poverty.

Livelihood...

Economic empowerment remains a cornerstone of OISD’s intervention strategies. The organization designs livelihood programs that provide income-generating opportunities, vocational training, and entrepreneurship support. By improving livelihoods, OISD addresses poverty and inequality, reduces vulnerability to conflict, and helps communities build resilience against economic shocks.

Education...

OISD believes that education is one of the most effective tools for sustainable peace and development. The organization supports initiatives that promote access to quality education for all, especially children in conflict-affected areas and disadvantaged communities. Programs include literacy promotion, school support, advocacy for educational reforms, and partnerships with educational institutions to ensure that learning becomes a transformative force for peace and empowerment.

Health...

Health is central to human dignity and development. OISD intervenes in health-related challenges through advocacy, awareness campaigns, and partnerships with health agencies. The organization addresses issues such as access to healthcare, maternal and child health, preventive medicine, mental health and health education. By promoting well-being, OISD ensures that individuals and communities are healthier, stronger, and more capable of contributing to national development.
