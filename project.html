Through a series of innovative, people-centered projects, we are empowering communities, restoring dignity, and driving progress across Nigeria and beyond.

Enhancing Waterways Safety

This six-year initiative will empower transport operators, local authorities, commuters, and communities with the tools and knowledge needed to prevent avoidable carnage on Nigeria's waterways. By promoting compliance with safety regulations, establishing early-warning systems, and building preparedness, Oneworld is setting new standards for maritime safety and emergency response.

Strengthening National Security Through Traditional Institutions

This four-year project will explore and enhance the critical role of traditional institutions in Nigeria's security framework. By recognizing their strengths and integrating them into national security strategies, Oneworld is building a bridge between tradition and modern governance for a safer, more united Nigeria.

Save Our Future Project

Launched in September 2021, this flagship initiative addresses the urgent challenge of out-of-school children across Nigeria and Africa. By providing scholarships, introducing a home-schooling model, and empowering parents with life skills for sustainability, the project has already begun to transform lives in the FCT, North Central, and across Nigeria's six geopolitical zones. Our goal: ensure that no child is left behind in the pursuit of education.

Reforming Third-Party Insurance and Protecting Rights on Our Roads

Since 2022, Oneworld has championed a six-year intervention project in the FCT to sanitize third-party motor insurance practices, restore the rights of road users, and ensure accident victims receive prompt medical care. This initiative safeguards citizens against abuse by enforcement agencies while reinforcing the right to safety, dignity, and justice on Nigeria's roads.
